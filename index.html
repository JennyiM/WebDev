My Favourite

Drosanthemum Stokoei (Variegated)

A branching green ice plant with showy purple flowers. Its leaves are finger shaped and bend and sprawl to access more light as they grow. The whole plant is covered in tiny hairs, the finest of which are on the leaves. This fine hair gives the leaves a dewy appearance while the longer hairs on the stem give the whole plant a fuzzy look.

Echeveria Berkeley Light

A genus of Echeveria in the Crassulaceae family about small and medium-sized variety. The shape of the plant is rosette-shaped, with a large round leaf tip and a small round leaf tail. When the plant is in good condition the leaves are white and tender with a small pink edge.
